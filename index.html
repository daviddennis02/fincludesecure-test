Services

Digital Payments

We support our client in defining, designing and the deployment of digital payment systems. This focuses on market demographics and the available alternative digital channels like USSD, mobile, internet, ATM etc to target these different identified market segments.

Digital Financial Services

We support our client in designing and deploying financial services and products best suited to their different market segments. These services and products includes but not limited to micro savings, micro loans, government to citizens products, agricultural & weather insurance, blockchain application products. The available digital channels giving the reach that customers require for scaling up while relying on private sector and regulator participation in ensuring that the required infrastructures are available per country to allow the thriving of Digital Financial Services.

Project Management & Delivery

We realize that customer requirement is rapidly changing, either due to market forces, competition or organization maturity. It is thus important to adopt a methodology that caters for this constant change while maintaining the delivery of minimal viable products in a fast paced world. We support and coach our client to become agile in their product and project deliveries thus giving speed and accuracy to meeting customer and market requirement..

Product Design

We support the definition and adoption of agile product management framework for and with our clients. Working with our clients, we help to create products in agriculture, banking, digital financial services that promotes financial inclusion ensuring simple and satisfactory user experience while deploying cutting edge technology.

Advisory and Strategy Consulting

We offer our client's C-Levels unbiased advice in the area of digital technology, using deep industry knowledge to deliver the best results and driving corporate goals.
Furthermore, we work with CTOs, CIOs and other corporate leaders, looking into business and operational support systems to reduce, manage, and mitigate technology and delivery risks by supporting improved processes and procedures in areas such as regulatory compliance, cyber security, and technology.

Market Research

Supporting our clients; on financial inclusion and the sustainable development goals (SDGs), we work with our clients for insight into markets and social impact of financial inclusion, electrification to excluded populations. This gives insights into the evolutions of payment from cash to digital in different countries and how regulators and governments are positioned to enable this developments.
